Virology Research Center

Laboratories play a critical role in surveillance, diagnosis and monitoring of viral disease as well as in the understanding of the genetic changes in the viral genome. Establishment of a reliable viral laboratory is a prerequisite for a strong public health response to emerging viral diseases. Unfortunately, several developing countries lack adequate capacity to diagnose viral infections. India is also one of those countries facing acute shortage of such facilities. The Design program intends to address the same issue. Emerging infectious diseases caused by viruses have assumed great public health significance in the recent past. During the last three decades, almost 20 new viral pathogens have been detected. Some of these–e.g. human immunodeficiency virus (HIV) and hepatitis viruses–have already caused substantial mortality, morbidity and economic loss all over the world. The pandemic of serious acute respiratory syndrome (SARS) unequivocally demonstrated the rapidity with which new viruses can travel across the world and inflict misery.

Mass Hosuing

Across the globe, urban populations have increased rapidly, often outstripping available housing. A century ago, one out of every five people reside in urban areas; now, for the first time in human history, more than half of us do. In these circumstances — and in many others brought about by various policies, laws and systems — one of the biggest obstacles is affordable housing. Pune experiences similar urbanisation challenges as other fast growing cities in developing countries: wide disparities in housing between the affluent, middle-income and low-income segments of the population.

Banni Landscape Project

Farmhouse landscape design Second homes or farmhouses play a role to give people a change from their daily routine, thus rejuvenating themselves. These spaces are far away from hustle and bustle of the city, are peaceful, serene and designed with a landscape. Further, Farm houses or second homes/bungalow landscapes are designed considering the surrounding context and site location. Thus concept of a farmhouse landscape is trying to weave the built with the unbuilt natural resources present in surroundings. This landscape not only serves as an intermediate connection between the two entities (built, unbuilt), but also serves as a purpose for an individual to nurture his own idea and purpose for landscape. Design brief encourages the idea to insert an appropriate landscape in a particular site. Consciously thinking of the resources present on site, climatic conditions that will give rise to an array of elements that will indeed go hand in hand with planting. The brief wants to address how planting in a landscape plays an important role and helps shape the design.
